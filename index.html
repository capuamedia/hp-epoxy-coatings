// 01 · Services

One standard.
Six ways to apply it.

Every job starts the same way — full mechanical grind, proper prep, no skipped steps. The finish depends on the floor. The process never changes.

01 / 06

Flake & Chip Systems

Our best-seller. Vinyl color chips broadcast into a 100% solids basecoat and sealed under polyaspartic — a textured, slip-resistant, factory finish in sixty stock blends or fully custom. The look most of our garages ask for by name.

Texture: Slip-resistant
Colors: 60+
Topcoat: Polyaspartic
02 / 06

Garage Floor Coatings

Full diamond grind, 100% solids epoxy basecoat, broadcast flake, polyaspartic topcoat. Built to shrug off hot tires and oil.

System: Multi-layer
Warranty: 5 yr
03 / 06

Metallic Epoxy

Showroom-grade pearlescent finish with hand-marbled effects. No two floors alike — depth like polished stone, performance of epoxy.

Depth: 3D
Style: Custom
04 / 06

Grind & Seal

A lower-investment option for slabs that need protection without the full coating system. We diamond-grind the surface and apply a penetrating sealer — a durable, natural-look finish that still starts with proper prep.

Finish: Natural look
Prep: Diamond grind
System: Single-layer
05 / 06

Polyaspartic Topcoat

We seal every floor with EasyFlo 88 VOC — a two-component, high-solids polyaspartic topcoat engineered for professional concrete flooring. UV-stable, chemical and abrasion resistant, with an extended working time that ensures a clean, even finish. Built for high-traffic environments and demanding installs.

UV: Stable
Wear: High abrasion resistance
Formula: High-solids
06 / 06

Crack Repair & Patchwork

Joint repair, crack chasing, and surface patchwork handled before any coating goes down. Available as a standalone service or as part of a full floor install.

Method: Mechanical
Bond: Permanent
Service: Standalone or bundled
